⚔️ Character Sheet Discord Bot

The Discord bridge for your DnD 5E Character Sheet.

Add to Discord

Why this Bot?

This bot connects your live character sheet directly to your Discord session. No more switching tabs to check your AC, roll a save, or verify how many spell slots you have left.

Available Commands

/roll Expression

Advanced dice parser. Supports math and complex notation.

/roll 2d6 + 5
Result: 12

/stats

A high-level overview of your character's ability scores, AC, and proficiency bonus.

/remaining Type

Check expendable resources. Autocompletes based on your class (Spells, Rages, Infusions, etc).

/say Message

Speak in-character using your sheet's portrait and name.

Y'un
"I suggest we don't touch the glowing orb."

/me Action

Describe an action. Formatted as a narrative scene description.

Y'un carefully examines the ancient runes.

/combat Name

Pulls data from your Weapons table. Features dynamic tab-complete for your arsenal.

/condition

Check active status effects with tactical commentary from the AI.

⚠️ Poisoned
> "Your internal systems are failing. How embarrassing."

/tip Topic

Get a tactical breakdown of D&D rules in the Dungeon Crawler Carl AI voice.

/register ID

Link your Discord account to a specific Character Sheet ID.

/register character_id:1234-abcd...
Result: Linked!

/help

Use this command in-server to quickly access this documentation page or to share the link with other players.

Skill & Ability Checks

Every standard D&D 5e skill is supported. Use the roll: true argument to automatically add your modifier to a d20 roll.

/perception roll:true
/stealth
/athletics roll:true

Get Help & Feedback

Running into issues? Want to request a feature?

  • Technical Support: Open an issue or leave a comment on our GitHub Repository.
  • Add Your Character: Use the new /register command! Simply copy your Character ID from the URL of your sheet (e.g., ?charId=THIS_PART) and run /register character_id:YOUR_ID in Discord.
